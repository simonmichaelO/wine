Our Story

Bringing Fine Wine Closer to You

Brown Rose was founded with a vision to bring refined, premium wine experiences to customers across Nigeria. As an authorized distributor of House of Brown Wines, we are committed to delivering quality, authenticity, and excellence in every bottle we provide.

What began as a passion for exceptional wines has grown into a trusted distribution network serving individuals, retailers, hospitality businesses, and corporate clients nationwide. We believe that great wine creates memorable moments, and we take pride in making world‑class selections accessible to our customers.

At Brown Rose, we don’t just distribute wine — we deliver quality, consistency, and a premium experience you can trust.
